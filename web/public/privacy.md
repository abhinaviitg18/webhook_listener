# Privacy Policy for AgentHook

**Last Updated: May 3, 2026**

Welcome to AgentHook. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure environment for your webhook and email processing.

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

### Webhook and Email Data
AgentHook processes incoming data payloads (webhooks and emails) sent to your listener URLs and inbox addresses.
- **Free Plan Usage**: For users on our Free Plan, data payloads and metadata may be accessed by the AgentHook engineering team for debugging, service improvement, troubleshooting, and platform optimization.
- **Privacy Guarantee**: We **never** share, sell, or rent your data payloads with third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or any other external entities. Your data remains your property.
- **Enterprise Plan**: For users requiring strict data privacy and zero-access guarantees, our Enterprise Plan offers single-tenant deployments where your data never leaves your own infrastructure.

### Usage Metrics
We track aggregate usage data, including but not limited to:
- Number of events processed per listener.
- Total event volume per account.
- Integration success/failure rates.

This data is used to maintain service reliability, manage system capacity, and communicate with you regarding service limits, potential plan upgrades, or relevant service updates.

### Account Information
We collect standard information required for authentication and service management, such as your email address and public alias.

## 2. Data Security
We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS.
- **Isolation**: Data is logically isolated between accounts.
- **Retention**: We store event data in the "Storyboard" to allow for reclassification and historical review. You can delete your account or specific data points at any time.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use reputable infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS, Cloudflare) to host our services. These providers are strictly used for infrastructure purposes and do not have access to your raw data except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., storage at rest).

## 4. Cookies
We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and manage your user experience. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website or through in-app notifications.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@agenthook.store.
